Grading Guide


  • Near Mint (NM)

    Near Mint cards appear fresh out of the pack.

  • Lightly Played (LP)

    May have a few edge or corner imperfections.

  • Moderately Played (MP)

    Visible wear, fading or border marks.

  • Heavily Played (HP)

    Noticeable damage, folding or dirt buildup.

  • Damaged (D)

    Major damage such as tears or ink/water stains.
